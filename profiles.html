Gintoki Sakata

Gintoki Sakata is the main character of the anime and manga series Gintama, created by Hideaki Sorachi. He’s a samurai living in a futuristic version of Edo, where aliens known as Amanto have taken over Japan. Gintoki, known for his silver hair and laid-back attitude, was once a legendary warrior during the Joi Wars, fighting against the Amanto. After the war, he became a freelancer, doing odd jobs to make ends meet.

Kagura Yato

Kagura is a member of one of the strongest and most feared warrior races in the universe. Despite her youthful appearance and innocent demeanor, Kagura possesses incredible physical strength, speed, and combat skills due to her Yato heritage. She has a bright personality, often seen as naive or childlike, but she’s also headstrong, quick to anger, and fiercely protective of her friends. Her appetite is almost insatiable, often eating in large quantities, and she has a comical weakness to sunlight due to her Yato blood.

Shinpachi Shimura

Shinpachi is the more grounded, "normal" member of the group, serving as a straight man to the absurd situations around him. He is a kind and responsible young man, often concerned with finances and keeping his friends in line. Shinpachi is highly loyal to Gintoki, whom he views as a mentor and older brother figure. While not as physically strong as Kagura or Gintoki, Shinpachi is skilled in swordsmanship and martial arts, continuing the dojo traditions of his late father.
